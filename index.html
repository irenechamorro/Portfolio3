GNRATION CLOTHING

DATE: Aug 2020 - Dec 2021

CATERGORIES: Graphic Design , Social Media Marketing

OVERVIEW: In this year long graphic design internship I worked with a motivational lifestyle clothing brand based

in Austin, making logos, clothing design, and social media advertisements.

I was asked to make a couple neutral colored variations of a new logo for the company.

The symbol above is a combination of the letter G and C, as requested.

WINTER COLLECTION

I was commissioned to draw out the logos for the winter collection released in

winter 2021. The collection included a sweatshirt set, T-shirts and a windbreaker. The insipration

behind the logo designs were making something light to match the pastel clothing theme.

FALL COLLECTION

I was commissioned to draw out the logos for the fall collection released in fall

2021, which included a shirt, two colors of shorts, and socks. The fall collection had more of a

streetwear vibe, and was more edgy than the first collection.
