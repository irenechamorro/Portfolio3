GNRATION CLOTHING

DATE: Aug 2020 - Dec 2021

CATERGORIES: Graphic Design , Social Media Marketing

OVERVIEW: In this year long graphic design internship I worked with a motivational lifestyle clothing

brand based in Austin, making logos, clothing design, and social media advertisements.

WINTER COLLECTION

I was commissioned to draw out the logos for the winter collection released in

winter 2021. The collection included a sweatshirt set, T-shirts and a windbreaker. The insipration

behind the logo designs were making something light to match the pastel clothing theme.

FALL COLLECTION

I was commissioned to draw out the logos for the fall collection released in fall

2021, which included a shirt, two colors of shorts, and socks. The fall collection had more of a

streetwear vibe, and was more edgy than the first collection.

ODD PHOBIAS

DATE: Sep 2021 - Oct 2021

CATERGORIES: Graphic Design , UI/UX

OVERVIEW: A website created to display a series of experiemental AR filters made with Snapchat's

Lens Studio. A parody of mobile apps that use augmenting exposure therapy for serious fears,

these AR experiences instead show humorous phobias such as Ephebiphobia, or fear of adolescents.

AR FILTERS

To display the AR filters on the webpage, I added clips of each experience on a interactive

phone image. Since the actual links to each experience are not available, this project would

probably be used as a website to promote a new AR game.
